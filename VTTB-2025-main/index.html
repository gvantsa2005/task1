About the Tournament

Veterans Table Tennis Championship

The Veterans Table Tennis Tournament in Batumi brings together experienced players from around the world to compete in a friendly yet competitive environment.

  • International Competition

    Compete against players from over 30 countries in various age categories and skill levels.

  • Veteran Categories

    Age groups include 40+, 50+, 60+, 70+, and 80+ for both men and women, ensuring fair competition.

  • Professional Organization

    Organized according to international standards with certified referees and high-quality equipment.

Tournament Details

Everything you need to know about the competition format, rules, and categories.

Competition Format

Group stage followed by knockout rounds. All matches best of 5 games to 11 points.

Categories

Singles, doubles, and mixed doubles across all age groups: 40+, 50+, 60+, 70+, and 80+.

Duration

6 days of competition from May 15-20, 2025, with opening ceremony on May 14.

Venue

Batumi Olympic Sports Palace, featuring 24 professional tables and spectator seating.

Equipment

ITTF-approved tables, nets, and flooring. Balls will be provided by the organizers.

Prizes

Medals, trophies, and certificates for winners and runners-up in all categories.

Venue Information

The tournament will be held at the Batumi Olympic Sports Palace.

Batumi Olympic Sports Palace

A state-of-the-art facility with excellent amenities for both players and spectators.

  • Address

    123 Olympic Avenue, Batumi, Georgia

  • Facilities

    24 competition tables, warm-up area, changing rooms, cafeteria, medical services, and spectator seating for 2,000 people.

  • Transportation

    10 minutes from Batumi city center. Shuttle service will be provided from official hotels.
